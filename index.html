Where is Resa

RESA is located in NOMA, the #1 most connected neighborhood in Washington DC.

Boasting a sleek design, bountiful amenities and exciting new features, RESA is not only a residential haven but it is an oasis of receation in the center of the neighborhood.

A large green urban landscape, called the Noma meander, starts between RESA at 22M street and Tyber place at 88M street. It extends from M street all the way to New York Ave. It will be a pedestrian crossing point through Noma. which will feature retail, services, and retail.
